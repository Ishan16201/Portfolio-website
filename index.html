A Brief Look at the Journey

Ishan Saraf is an 18-year-old BSc Economics student with a unique blend of academic rigour and hands-on market experience. His journey began with trading in the complex Indian equity and commodity markets, quickly expanding to global instruments like XAUUSD (Gold) and major Forex pairs.

He is the author of two published finance books and a book on trading psychology, demonstrating a commitment to synthesizing complex ideas into accessible knowledge. As an algorithmic developer, Ishan successfully built a profitable Bitcoin algo, focusing his quantitative research on core concepts like liquidity, macroeconomics, and market structure.

Curious, disciplined, and unfazed by complex challenges, Ishan aims to bridge the gap between economic theory and high-performance quantitative practice.
